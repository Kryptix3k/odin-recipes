Lasagna Recipe

Garfield's favorite food and a classic for everyone!

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Sugar
  • Cheeses and Egg

Recipe Instructions

  1. Make the Meat Sauce
  2. Cook the Noodles and make Ricotta
  3. Layer the lasagna
  4. Cover with foil and bake, then rest
